2. retail sales dashboard
   ```
   A quick question, why doesn't revenue growth consistently translate into profit growth?
   Pricing (or discount) strategy, (structural) margin inefficiencies, and what else?

   Using a dummy sales dataset, I made two Tableau dashboards, one contains executive summary
   while the other contains a simple analysis on product & profitability, and then found:
   - Revenue and profit unalignment
   - Regional performance imbalance
   - Technology category as a growth engine
   - Some margin risk categories
 
   Check these out:
   - https://irdazh.github.io/posts/retail-sales-dashboard/
   ```
